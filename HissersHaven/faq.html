About Our Bugs

Tropical Roaches vs Pests

Tropical Madagascar Hissing Cockroaches require, well, tropical conditions to thrive.

  • Hissers require heat and humidity in order to breed.
  • Hissers can only survive off a diet of fresh fruit and store-bought insect feed.
  • Hisser's Haven can easily determine gender even in juveniles! Simply choose a specific gender in the order menu and your pets will never become a colony under ANY circumstances.

Are they friendly?

Yes! Hissing cockroaches do not bite or sting, and can be handled with ease.

What's the point?

Here at Hisser's Haven our goal is to offer a tank-alternative to fish; offering an equally colorful and unique set of co-habitable creatures that can live in a low-maintenance enclosure.
