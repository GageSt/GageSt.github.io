Getting Started

Hissing Roaches require a tank with no openings, tropical moisture, and a heat source

General Care

  • Hissing Roaches occasionally molt and shouldn't be touched or held during this time.

Breeding

Gendering your Roach

A male roach will have an additional abdominal plate on the end of their exoskeleton where the female will only have one.

Courtship

One gendered pair of hissers can produce generational colonies in the hundreds as long as the care standards are met.
