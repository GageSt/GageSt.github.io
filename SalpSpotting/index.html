Diving Down Into the World of Salps

Salps, frequently referred to as "Sea Squirts" are ocean-dwelling relatives of plankton that reach impressive sizes. These unique creatures live throughout the ocean; some near the equator responding to algae blooms, but mostly in the Southern Ocean living in large Atlantic colonies.
